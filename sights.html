Stay With Us
Burke
4 miles away from Captain Zippy's. Enjoy golfing. Famous for its Burke Stampede Rodeo held every July. Come for the party for Dog Days in early August.
Burke Lake
3.7 miles from Captain Zippy's. Enjoy fishing, hiking, picnicking, camping and boating.
Bonesteel

15.9 miles east of Captain Zippy's. Make your way here to go swimming in their public outdoor swimming pool, or find one of the many routes to the wide Missouri River to enjoy swimming, boating and fishing.

Gregory
12 miles from Captain Zippy's. Go bowling, watch a movie (depending on the season - indoor or drive-in), four restaurants and a thirty minute drive from Lake Francis Case on the Missouri River.

Burke Stampede Rodeo

Come see the famous Burke Stampede Rodeo in July and take part in the dirtiest vehicle competition as well as the Stampede Idol Contest!

Burke Dog Days

Come enjoy Dog Days in Burke every early August!
